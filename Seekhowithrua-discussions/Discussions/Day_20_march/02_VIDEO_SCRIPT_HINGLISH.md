### 🎬 SEGMENT 2: THE EVIDENCE — RESEARCH DROP (1:00–2:30)

**[ANIMATION ONLY — Master Rua voiceover — 1:00–2:30]**

**[VOICEOVER]:**
> "Mere paas ek opinion nahi hai. Mere paas proof hai. Real research. Peer-reviewed papers. Sunno."

---

**[RESEARCH CARD 1 — 1:08]**
*Animation: Forgetting curve graph draws itself — steeply dropping.*

> "Hermann Ebbinghaus — 1885 mein yeh discover hua, 2015 mein PLOS ONE ne confirm kiya — jo aap aaj class mein seekha, uska **70% agle 24 ghante mein bhool jaoge.** Ek hafte mein? **90% gone.** Passive learning literally brain mein stick nahi karta."

*Source badge:* Ebbinghaus (1885) · Murre & Dros, PLOS ONE 2015

---

**[RESEARCH CARD 2 — 1:24]**
*Animation: Two brains side by side — one dull watching a lecture, one lit up playing a game.*

> "89 studies. 17 countries. Educational Research Review 2023. Result? **Gamified learning ne knowledge retention 40% tak badha diya** compared to traditional instruction. Games are not distraction — games ARE the learning."

*Source badge:* Dichev & Dicheva, Educational Research Review 2023

---

**[RESEARCH CARD 3 — 1:40]**
*Animation: Two glowing memory pathways branching from a brain — visual + verbal combining.*

> "Allan Paivio ki Dual Coding Theory — jab tum visual aur verbal information saath mein process karte ho, toh brain mein **do memory pathways** banti hain. Result? **6 guna zyada retention.** Yahi reason hai 3D animation-based learning itna powerful hai."

*Source badge:* Paivio (1991), J. Educational Psychology · Mayer (2009), Cambridge University Press

---

**[RESEARCH CARD 4 — 1:55]**
*Animation: A glowing memory palace — a person walking through rooms, placing numbers on objects.*

> "2017. Neuron journal. Dresler et al. ne prove kiya — **sirf 40 days ki memory training se ordinary people World Memory Championship level performance achieve kar sakte hain.** 72 words, sequence mein, perfectly. Ye koi gift nahi — yeh technique hai."

*Source badge:* Dresler et al., Neuron 93(5), 2017

---

**[RESEARCH CARD 5 — 2:08]**
*Animation: India map. 1.5 million engineer icons appear. Then 75% fade to grey.*

> "India har saal **1.5 million engineers** produce karta hai. NASSCOM-McKinsey report 2023 kehti hai — **sirf 25% hireable hain** tech roles ke liye. Problem intelligence nahi hai. Problem yeh hai ki hum **sikhate kaise hain**."

*Source badge:* NASSCOM-McKinsey Employability Report 2023 · AICTE Annual Report 2024

---

**[RESEARCH CARD 6 — 2:18]**
*Animation: Body in motion — Kung Fu stance — neural sparks firing in brain.*

> "British Journal of Sports Medicine 2019 — physical training like dance, martial arts, sports **directly improve neuroplasticity, working memory, aur cognitive speed by up to 30%.** Body aur brain ek hi system hai. Alag nahi."

*Source badge:* Hillman et al., British Journal of Sports Medicine 2019
