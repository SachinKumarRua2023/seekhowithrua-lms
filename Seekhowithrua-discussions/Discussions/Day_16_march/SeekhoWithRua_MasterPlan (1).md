## Part 1 — Legal Setup (Month 1, Cost: ~₹2,000–5,000)

### Do you need certification to teach or do seminars?
**NO.** In India, anyone can offer a guest lecture or workshop. No government body, no certification, no registration required to speak at colleges or train professionals. Your proof is your work — teaching US professionals data science every night IS your credential.

### Business Registration (Do in this order)
1. **Sole Proprietorship** — Start here. Free to set up. Use your own PAN. Open a current bank account in the name "SeekhoWithRua". Cost: ₹500–2,000 for bank account.
2. **Udyam MSME Certificate** — Register free at udyamregistration.gov.in. Gives you government benefits, easier bank loans, credibility with colleges and investors.
3. **GST Registration** — Only needed once revenue crosses ₹20 lakhs/year. Skip for now.
4. **Trademark** — Register "SeekhoWithRua" at ipindia.gov.in (₹4,500 for individuals). Do this in Month 2 before someone else takes it.
5. **Upgrade to LLP or Pvt Ltd** — Only when you raise angel funding (around Month 10–12). Investors require this.

### Domain & Brand
- Register seekhowithrua.com immediately (GoDaddy/Namecheap ~₹800/year)
- Create @seekhowithrua on Instagram, LinkedIn, YouTube, Twitter — even if empty for now

## Part 12 — Hybrid Class Format

### Structure: 3 Days Per Week · 18 Weeks

| Day | Format | Duration | Content |
|---|---|---|---|
| Monday | Online (Google Meet) | 90 min | New concept taught — recorded automatically |
| Wednesday | Online (Google Meet) | 60 min | Doubt clearing + code review + assignment given |
| Saturday | In-person (lab) | 3 hours | Hands-on project building + team work |

### Why hybrid beats pure online and pure offline
- Pure online: 8–15% completion rate. Students disappear.
- Pure offline: Limited to one city. Caps at room size.
- Hybrid: 60–75% completion rate. Online = national reach. Saturday = accountability and community.

### Where to run Saturday sessions in Noida
1. **Partner college computer lab** — free. Offer them priority enrollment discount for their students in return.
2. **Co-working space** — IndiQube, 91springboard Noida — Rs 200–500/day.
3. **Your home** — 10–15 students max. Most personal and powerful.

### Running two parallel batches
- Batch A: Monday morning + Wednesday morning + Saturday morning
- Batch B: Tuesday evening + Thursday evening + Saturday evening
- 2 batches x 20 students x Rs 5,999 = Rs 2.4 lakhs per 18-week cycle
- 2 cycles per year = Rs 4.8 lakhs · Scale online to 50 students = Rs 12 lakhs/year

### Recording system
Record every Monday and Wednesday session (OBS or Zoom). Upload to private YouTube playlist same evening. Students who miss can catch up. After 3 batches you have 100+ recorded sessions — this becomes SeekhoWithRua's automated course library.

### Weekly rhythm for student
- Monday: Learn new concept (90 min live)
- Tue–Wed: Practice independently + small assignment
- Wednesday evening: Doubt clearing + code review (60 min)
- Thu–Fri: Complete assignment
- Saturday: In-person lab — build project piece (3 hours)
- Sunday: Rest. Ready for Monday.
