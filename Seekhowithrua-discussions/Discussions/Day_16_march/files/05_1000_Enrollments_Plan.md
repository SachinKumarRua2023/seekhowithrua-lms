## Channel 5 — Referral Program

**Simple rule:** Refer 1 friend who enrolls = get Rs 500 back via UPI.

**Why it works:**
- Student paid Rs 5,999, refers 2 friends → effectively paid Rs 4,999
- You spent Rs 1,000 to acquire 2 students worth Rs 11,998
- 12x return on referral spend

**How to manage:** WhatsApp message thread. Student sends "I referred [name]." When referred friend pays, you transfer Rs 500 via UPI same day.

**This is why batch 1 quality matters more than batch 1 revenue.**
