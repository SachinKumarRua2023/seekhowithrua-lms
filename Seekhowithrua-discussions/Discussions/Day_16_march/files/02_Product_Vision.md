# SeekhoWithRua — Product Vision
*AI Gamified Learning Ecosystem*

---

## One-Line Definition

SeekhoWithRua is a strictly learning-focused AI gamified ecosystem — voice rooms, battle games, English practice, expert trainers, hiring — with zero tolerance for entertainment beyond 30 minutes per day.

## 5 Core Pillars

### Pillar 1 — AI Learning Engine
- Visual animated courses with 3D diagrams
- 100+ mnemonic techniques (memory palace, major system, PAO, spaced repetition)
- Adaptive AI tutor — knows each student's weak areas
- Personalised quiz generation from performance data
- Hindi + English + Hinglish support

### Pillar 2 — Battle Games
- Two students enter, answer same questions simultaneously
- Correct answer = attack power, wrong answer = lose HP
- Skill trees — completing modules unlocks new branches
- XP points, levels, daily streaks, leaderboards
- Clan system — study groups compete in team hackathons

### Pillar 3 — Voice Rooms (heart of platform)

| Room Type | Purpose | Time limit |
|---|---|---|
| Learning room | Expert teaches live | Unlimited |
| English practice | Speaking + AI correction live | Unlimited |
| Hackathon room | Team builds in 48 hours | Unlimited |
| Mentorship room | 1-on-1 paid session | Unlimited |
| Fun activity room | Relaxation only | 30 min/day MAX — hard AI lock |

**English AI Correction:** Whisper API transcribes speech → GPT-4 flags grammar → popup correction shown to speaker → trainer sees all corrections in dashboard.

### Pillar 4 — Hiring + Projects
- Verified skill profiles (quiz scores + deployed projects)
- Hiring rooms — company notifies matching students directly
- Hackathon hosting for brands with live leaderboard
- GitHub + live deploy project showcase

### Pillar 5 — Creator Economy
- 1,000 followers = monetisation unlocks
- Trainers earn 70% of course revenue
- Live donations in voice rooms
- Sponsored course hosting

## Platform Rules — Non-Negotiable

### Allowed (Unlimited)
- All learning rooms
- English practice rooms
- Hackathon and project rooms
- Mentorship rooms
- Innovative idea sharing rooms

### Strictly Blocked
- Dating, flirting, romantic conversation — any room
- Singing, entertainment, non-learning content
- Fun rooms beyond 30 minutes (AI auto-locks)
- Off-topic chatter in learning rooms (AI flag after 3 minutes)
- Any content not related to learning, skills or career

### Enforcement — No Human Moderator Needed
- AI monitors rooms via keyword detection and topic drift analysis
- 3 off-topic flags = host loses room privileges for 7 days
- Community self-polices — bad hosts get reported by students
- Parents and colleges trust it because discipline is built into the design

## The 30-Minute Fun Rule — Your Brand Identity

Say this in every seminar:

> "SeekhoWithRua is the only platform that forces you to learn. After 30 minutes of fun, the AI locks the room and puts you back in a learning room. Because we respect your time more than you do right now."

Students laugh. Then they think. Then they enroll — because somewhere inside every student is someone who WANTS to be disciplined but cannot do it alone. You give them a system that does the discipline for them.
