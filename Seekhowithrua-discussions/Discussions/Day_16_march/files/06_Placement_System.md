# Student Placement System
*No HCL connections needed. AI does 80% of the work.*

---

## The Honest Truth About Placement

You do not need HCL or Infosys. Those companies pay freshers Rs 3.5–4.5 lakhs and put them in service projects maintaining old Java code. Your students will be miserable there.

**Target: Funded startups with 15–150 people.**

These companies:
- Cannot afford senior engineers
- Are building real products with modern tech
- Hire on Wellfound, Internshala, LinkedIn Easy Apply
- Respond to cold DMs from candidates with real projects
- Value a deployed AI project over a degree certificate

## The System — AI Does 80%, You Do 20%

### Step 1 — Resume (5 minutes per student)

Student fills this Google Form:
- Full name
- Skills (Python, React, Django, etc.)
- Projects built during course (name + GitHub link + live URL)
- Target role (frontend / backend / full stack / data / AI)
- College and graduation year

You paste into Claude:
> "Generate a clean ATS-friendly one-page resume for a fresher developer with these details: [paste form response]"

Claude produces resume in 2 minutes. You review and approve in 5 minutes. Student gets professional resume.

---

### Step 2 — Daily Application System (Student Does This Themselves)

Give every student this daily checklist:

```
Every morning — 45 minutes:
□ 10 applications on Internshala
□ 5 LinkedIn Easy Apply
□ 3 cold DMs to startup founders on LinkedIn
□ Update application tracker spreadsheet
```

**Cold DM script — give this exact text to students:**

> "Hi [Name], I recently completed an 18-week AI + full stack course at SeekhoWithRua and built [project name] — a [one line description using Django + React + PostgreSQL]. I noticed [company] is working on [relevant thing]. I'd love to contribute as an intern or junior developer. Here is my project: [GitHub link] [live URL]."

This message works. Startup founders respond. It is specific, shows skill, shows initiative.

---

### Step 3 — LinkedIn Profile (One-time setup)

**Headline formula:**
> "Fresher | AI + Full Stack Developer | Built [X project] | SeekhoWithRua Alumni"

**About section:**
> "I completed an 18-week Full Stack AI Builder course at SeekhoWithRua where I built [project]. I learned Python, React, Django, PostgreSQL, AI integration, IoT basics, and deployment. Looking for junior developer or internship opportunities."

**Weekly post routine:**
> "Week [X] update — I built [something] today using [tech]. Here is what I learned: [2–3 lines]. [screenshot]"

**You repost every student's post from your account.** Your LinkedIn becomes a live showcase of students building real things. Recruiters follow you. They DM you asking to hire students. You become the connection.

---

### Step 4 — Mock Interview (30 minutes per premium student)

**Students use ChatGPT daily:**
> "Interview me for a junior Python/Django developer role. Ask me 10 technical questions one by one and give feedback after each answer."

**You do one live mock per premium student** — WhatsApp video call, 30 minutes. Record it. Review together. This justifies the Rs 1,500 placement addon price.
