## 5 Entry Points — Use in This Order

### Entry 1 — Email TPO (40% yes rate)
Training and Placement Officer is your first contact. They care about one thing: placement numbers.

**Subject line:**
> Free AI/ML Industry Session for Your Students — Placement Focused

**Email body — use exactly:**
> Dear [Name],
>
> I train working professionals in the United States in AI and data science daily from Noida. I want to offer your students a free 45-minute hands-on session — "How to Build and Deploy an AI App in 2026."
>
> No theory. Live demo. Students leave with something they actually built. I only need a projector and 45 minutes. No fees charged.
>
> My platform SeekhoWithRua has helped students get placed in AI and web development roles.
>
> Can we fix a date this week?
>
> Warm regards,
> [Your name]
> seekhowithrua.com | [phone]

---

### Entry 2 — Visit HOD in Person (60% yes rate)
Walk in. Dress professionally. Ask for HOD of CS/IT or TPO directly.

**What to say in 10 minutes:**
> "Sir, I currently train working professionals in the United States in AI and data science — every night from Noida. I want to bring that same quality of training to your students. I am proposing a partnership where I run a free 45-minute session first. If students respond well, I can offer a full 18-week course. I only need your computer lab on Saturday mornings for 3 hours. In return, your students get a 20% discount, the certificate carries your college name alongside SeekhoWithRua, and I will do a free seminar every semester. No money exchanged. Pure value for your students."

---

### Entry 3 — LinkedIn DM to Professor (30% yes rate)
Search "[college name] professor computer science" on LinkedIn. Send:

> "Hi [Name], I am a data science trainer who teaches US professionals nightly from Noida. I would love to offer your students a free session on AI careers in 2026. Would you be open to a 45-minute guest lecture for your class? No fees — just value for your students."

---

### Entry 4 — Student Tech Club President (70% yes rate — FASTEST)
Best entry point. Student club presidents are hungry, have direct WhatsApp access to 200–500 students, and can organise without bureaucratic approval.

Find them on LinkedIn or Instagram by searching "[college name] tech club" or "[college name] coding club."

**DM:**
> "Hey! I train US professionals in AI nightly. Want me to do a free session for your tech club? Live demo — students will actually build something. Can do it any day this week."

He says yes within hours. You get 50+ students in one session.

---

### Entry 5 — Tech Fest Speaker (80% yes rate)
Every engineering college has a technical festival between October and March. Search "[college name] techfest 2026" and find their speaker application form.

**Submit as:**
> Industry Expert — AI/ML Trainer, US Client Experience, Building India's First AI Gamified Learning Platform (SeekhoWithRua)

500–2,000 students in one room. One fest = 50–100 enrollments in a single day.

## The 45-Minute Seminar Script

| Time | What you do |
|---|---|
| 0–5 min | Your story — village → kung fu → films → teaching USA professionals |
| 5–15 min | Live demo — open Claude/ChatGPT, build something real in 10 minutes |
| 15–25 min | The AI era truth — "basics of everything + AI = more employable than 4-year engineer" |
| 25–35 min | Show SeekhoWithRua — gamified learning, course structure, student projects |
| 35–40 min | Placement proof — "Our students get placed. Here is how." |
| 40–45 min | QR code for WhatsApp + early bird enrollment Rs 3,999 (48 hours only) |

### The Line That Closes the Room
> "Engineering colleges charge Rs 8 lakhs for 4 years and give you theory. I charge Rs 5,999 for 18 weeks and give you a deployed product and a job. You decide."

### The Live Demo (Most Important Part)
Open your laptop. Go to Claude or ChatGPT. In 10 minutes build something real — a Python script, a working webpage, a data visualisation. Students go silent. Then you say "I will teach you to do this in Week 1." That silence turns into 20 hands asking how to enroll.

## Three Scenarios When Approaching

**College says yes immediately (40%)** — Sign MOU, start Saturday sessions next week.

**College says "let us think" (40%)** — Follow up every 5 days. Offer a free no-commitment demo session first. Usually converts in 2–3 weeks.

**College says no (20%)** — Move on immediately. 80+ colleges in Noida/Greater Noida. Even 30% yes rate = 24 partners. You need only 2–3 to run comfortably.
